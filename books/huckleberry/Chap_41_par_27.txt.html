“My very _words_, Brer Penrod  I was a-sayin'--pass that-air sasser o' m'lasses, won't ye --I was a-sayin' to Sister Dunlap, jist this minute, how _did_ they git that grindstone in there, s'I  Without _help_, mind you--'thout _help_  _that's_ wher 'tis  Don't tell _me_, s'I; there _wuz_ help, s'I; 'n' ther' wuz a _plenty_ help, too, s'I; ther's ben a _dozen_ a-helpin' that nigger, 'n' I lay I'd skin every last nigger on this place but _I'd_ find out who done it, s'I; 'n' moreover, s'I--”
