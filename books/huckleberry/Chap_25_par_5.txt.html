When we got to the house the street in front of it was packed, and the three girls was standing in the door  Mary Jane _was_ red-headed, but that don't make no difference, she was most awful beautiful, and her face and her eyes was all lit up like glory, she was so glad her uncles was come The king he spread his arms, and Mary Jane she jumped for them, and the hare-lip jumped for the duke, and there they had it  Everybody most, leastways women, cried for joy to see them meet again at last and have such good times
