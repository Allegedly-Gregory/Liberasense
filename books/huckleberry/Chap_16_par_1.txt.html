WE slept most all day, and started out at night, a little ways behind a monstrous long raft that was as long going by as a procession  She had four long sweeps at each end, so we judged she carried as many as thirty men, likely  She had five big wigwams aboard, wide apart, and an open camp fire in the middle, and a tall flag-pole at each end  There was a power of style about her  It _amounted_ to something being a raftsman on such a craft as that
