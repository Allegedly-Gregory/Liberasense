Then the ringmaster he see how he had been fooled, and he _was_ the sickest ringmaster you ever see, I reckon  Why, it was one of his own men  He had got up that joke all out of his own head, and never let on to nobody Well, I felt sheepish enough to be took in so, but I wouldn't a been in that ringmaster's place, not for a thousand dollars  I don't know; there may be bullier circuses than what that one was, but I never struck them yet Anyways, it was plenty good enough for _me_; and wherever I run across it, it can have all of _my_ custom every time
