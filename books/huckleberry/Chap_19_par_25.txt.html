“Yes, it _is_ good enough for me; it's as good as I deserve; for who fetched me so low when I was so high  I did myself  I don't blame _you_, gentlemen--far from it; I don't blame anybody  I deserve it all  Let the cold world do its worst; one thing I know--there's a grave somewhere for me The world may go on just as it's always done, and take everything from me--loved ones, property, everything; but it can't take that Some day I'll lie down in it and forget it all, and my poor broken heart will be at rest ”  He went on a-wiping
