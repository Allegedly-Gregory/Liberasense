I got to feeling so mean and so miserable I most wished I was dead  I fidgeted up and down the raft, abusing myself to myself, and Jim was fidgeting up and down past me  We neither of us could keep still  Every time he danced around and says, “Dah's Cairo ” it went through me like a shot, and I thought if it _was_ Cairo I reckoned I would die of miserableness
